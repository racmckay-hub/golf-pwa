📖 How to Use the Golf Swing Analyzer

🎯 Before You Start

The quality of your video directly affects the analysis accuracy.

  • ❌ Portrait videos (tall) will NOT work correctly
  • ❌ Videos shorter than 8 seconds will NOT capture the full swing
  • ✅ Landscape videos (wide) give the best results
  • ✅ 8-12 second videos capture the complete swing

🎯 What This Tool Does

Our AI-powered analyzer compares your golf swing to professional golfers and provides detailed feedback to help you improve.

📹 Video Requirements

  • Camera Position: Film from "Down the Line" (behind the golfer, facing the target)
  • Orientation: LANDSCAPE ONLY – hold your phone horizontally
  • Duration: At least 8-10 seconds to capture the full swing
  • Full Body: Ensure your entire body and the club are visible
  • Lighting: Use good lighting (outdoor or bright indoor)
  • Frame Rate: 30fps or 60fps recommended
  • Format: MP4, MOV, or AVI (max 100MB)

✅ Example of a Good Video:

  • Landscape (wide) orientation
  • 8-10 seconds long
  • Full body visible from head to toes
  • Club visible throughout the swing
  • Good lighting, no shadows on the golfer
  • Camera at waist height, behind the golfer

📊 What You'll Get

  • Overall Score: 0-100 rating of your swing
  • Key Metrics: Spine angle, hip rotation, X-factor, and more
  • Pro Comparison: See how you compare to Rory McIlroy, Tiger Woods, and others
  • Personalized Recommendations: Specific drills and swing thoughts
  • Grade: A+ to D based on your performance

⏱️ How Long It Takes

The analysis takes about 30 seconds after you upload your video. You'll see results immediately.
