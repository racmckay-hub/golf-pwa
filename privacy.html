🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What We Collect

  • Email address: For account creation and login.
  • Video uploads: Temporarily uploaded for analysis. Videos are deleted immediately after processing.
  • Analysis data: Metrics, scores, and recommendations are saved to your account for tracking progress.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide swing analysis and recommendations.
  • To track your improvement over time.
  • To improve our AI models (anonymized data only).

3. Data Security

Your data is stored securely in a cloud database. Video files are stored temporarily and deleted immediately after analysis.

4. Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
  • You can opt out of any future communications.

5. Contact
